Page Monitor

Privacy Policy

We believe in radical transparency. Here's exactly what we do (and don't do) with your data.

📅 Effective date: January 1, 2025

✅ TL;DR: Page Monitor stores all data locally on your device only. We collect zero personal information, send nothing to external servers, and never share your data with anyone.

01 Overview

Page Monitor ("the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension that allows users to monitor changes on web pages and receive notifications when content changes. This Privacy Policy describes how the Extension handles information.

This policy applies to the Page Monitor Chrome extension available on the Chrome Web Store.

02 Data We Collect

We collect no personal data. The Extension operates entirely on your local device.

The following information is stored locally on your device only using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API:

  • URLs of web pages you choose to monitor
  • CSS selectors you configure for monitored elements
  • Text content snapshots of monitored elements (for change detection)
  • Your notification and check interval preferences
  • History of detected changes

This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

03 Permissions Explained

The Extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

Permission Why it's needed
activeTab To read content from the current tab when you add a monitor or check for changes
storage To save your monitor list and change history locally on your device
alarms To schedule periodic checks at the interval you configure
notifications To send you browser notifications when a change is detected
scripting To extract text content from monitored elements on web pages
tabs To access the URL of the current tab when setting up a monitor
host_permissions (<all_urls>) Required to read page content on any URL you choose to monitor

04 Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. We have no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no external API calls of any kind.

  • No data is sent to our servers (we have none)
  • No third-party analytics or tracking SDKs are included
  • No advertising networks have access to any information
  • No data is sold or licensed to any party

05 Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored locally and you have full control:

  • Delete individual monitors at any time from the extension popup
  • Clear all change history with one click
  • Uninstalling the extension permanently removes all stored data

06 Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

07 Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

08 Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository or contact us at the email listed on the Chrome Web Store listing.
